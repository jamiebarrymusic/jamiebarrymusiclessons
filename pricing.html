FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to own an instrument to start? ▾
Not necessarily — reach out and we can discuss options. For guitar students especially it's helpful to have something to practice on at home, but we can talk through affordable starter setups.
What age groups do you teach? ▾
13 years old is the ideal starting age for full 45-minute sessions. For younger students, reach out to discuss shorter lesson formats and adjusted pricing that works better for their attention span and development.
How do I pay for lessons? ▾
Payment is due at the end of each lesson. All forms of payment are accepted — Venmo, check, or cash are the most convenient.
What's your cancellation policy? ▾
Life happens — just give as much notice as possible if you need to reschedule. A full session policy will be discussed when you book your first lesson.
Can I do both guitar and drums? ▾
Absolutely! Many students explore multiple instruments. We'd just schedule separate lesson slots for each — reach out to discuss what that might look like.
